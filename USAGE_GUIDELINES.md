## Overview
The **PersonaX datasets** (CelebPersona and AthlePersona) are released for **non-commercial, academic research purposes only**. They are designed to support the study of public behavioral traits, multimodal analysis, and causal reasoning in a responsible and ethical manner.

## Permitted Use
- Academic research, teaching, and experimentation.  
- Development of algorithms or methods for **causal interpretation**, **representation learning**, or **multimodal analysis**.  
- Exploration of public behavioral traits in a manner that respects individual dignity, diversity, and fairness.  

## Prohibited Use
You may **NOT** use these datasets, or any models derived from them, for:  
- High-stakes or consequential decision-making (e.g., **employment, credit scoring, insurance, education, medical, or legal decisions**).  
- Surveillance, monitoring, or tracking of individuals.  
- Any form of **automated personality profiling**, behavioral targeting, or commercial exploitation.  
- Generating or inferring **private, sensitive, or clinical psychological information**.  

## Privacy and Anonymization
- No raw images or textual descriptions are released. Instead, all data is provided as high-dimensional embeddings with additional transformations to mitigate re-identification risks.  
- Names and personally identifiable information (PII) have been removed or obfuscated.  
- Users must not attempt to reverse-engineer or re-identify individuals from the embeddings.  

## Ethical Responsibilities
By accessing or using these datasets, you agree to:  
- Respect the **consent-based, non-commercial license** under which the data is provided.  
- Prioritize fairness, transparency, and privacy in all analyses.  
- Clearly acknowledge and cite the original source when using the datasets in publications or derived works.  
- Consider dataset **biases and limitations** (e.g., male-only athletes in \texttt{AthlePersona}, wealth/visibility bias in \texttt{CelebPersona}) and avoid overgeneralization of findings.  

## Disclaimer
The PersonaX datasets are intended solely for **research and educational purposes**. The authors make no representations or warranties regarding the accuracy or fitness of the data for any particular use. Users bear full responsibility for ensuring their research complies with ethical standards, institutional requirements, and applicable laws.
